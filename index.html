Choosing a college? Brown College Consulting will help you find your fit!

There are several moving pieces that sometimes make the college search process confusing and frustrating. Our team will give you the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the college process with confidence and joy. After all, these are the best years of your life!

Why choose BCC?

Find the Right College

Not every college is for every student. We strive to help you make an informed decision about which college to go to. Choosing a college that does not fit means wasting thousands of dollars and priceless time. Let our team help find the best college for your student.

Personal Service

You and your family will have the luxury of a personal touch in your college search. Our team will get to know you personally and be able to help your student find a college where they will thrive academically and socially.

Reduce Stress

The whole college application process can be stressful. Let us do research on colleges, help keep track of application deadlines, and walk you through the entire process. Make this experience more enjoyable and less stressful for you and your family.

Meet the Team

Matthew Brown has been working in youth ministry for the past five years. During that time, he has seen how stressful choosing a college can be. He is passionate about the college experience and believes it has great benefits for students academically and socially. He wants to help students find where they belong. Matthew obtained his undergrad degrees in Music, Religion and Youth Ministry from Hope College in Holland, MI. He is currently working on a Masters of the Arts in Family, Youth and Children's Ministries at Wesley Seminary in Marion, IN.



Services

Brown College Consulting offers a variety of services on an hourly basis.

Deciding a Major: Is your student not sure what to major in? Let Brown College Consulting help! We get to know your student’s interests and passions in order to help guide them to a major that the student is excited about.

Finding the Right College For You: With over 4,000 colleges and universities to choose from, it can be hard to narrow it down to a short list. Let us get to know your student and research the colleges that are the best fit.

Visits and Interviews: Visits and college interviews are an important part of the college choosing process. Brown College Consulting can help give tips and resources in order for you to get the most out of your college visits.

College Applications: BCC assisted applications stand out!

Scholarships: With the rising cost of college, scholarships can be a great way to reduce the amount of money spent. However, many people do not have the time to find all of the scholarships available pay less for college!

Final College Decision: Brown College Consulting can help talk through the final college decision to make sure your student picks the college the is the best fit.

Need all of these services? We offer package deals that include all of these services!

Deluxe Package

This package offers time-based consulting for students looking for comprehensive assistance. This process will be customized to tailor towards your individual goals. We provide up to 20 hours for juniors and seniors and up to 25 hours for seniors and juniors.

Elite Package

This package is an all inclusive program with no time cap for students and parents. You will get the benefit of priority scheduling, no cap on time, extra research assistance and support to manage all aspects of the college process including final college choice.

Transfer Students

Are you a college student looking to transfer? We can help with that too! Let us help you make a smooth transition to the next step in your education!
